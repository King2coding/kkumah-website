About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Arizona's Department of Hydrology and Atmospheric Sciences. My expertise lies in remote sensing-based precipitation retrieval, focusing on algorithm development, diagnostic evaluation, and uncertainty analysis. My research integrates data from multiple sources—including infrared (IR), microwave remote sensing, in situ observations, and reanalysis data—to enhance precipitation measurement accuracy. Currently, I am developing an AVHRR-based precipitation product targeting global high-latitude regions, complementing efforts like GPCP and IMERG.
